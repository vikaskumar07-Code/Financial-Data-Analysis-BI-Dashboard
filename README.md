# 📊 Financial Analyst Project – End-to-End Financial Data Analysis & BI Dashboard

## Project Overview

This project is an end-to-end **Financial Analytics & Business Intelligence solution** built to analyze company fundamentals, identify investment patterns, evaluate financial health, and generate stakeholder-driven insights.

The project combines **Python (Data Engineering), SQL (Business Analysis), and Power BI (Visualization & Storytelling)** to transform raw financial statements into actionable business intelligence.

The final output includes:

* Cleaned Financial Master Dataset
* SQL-based Stakeholder Analysis
* Executive Reports
* Interactive Power BI Dashboard

---

## Business Problem

Financial data is often fragmented across multiple statements and difficult to interpret for investment or strategic decision-making.

This project solves that problem by creating:

✔ Unified Financial Dataset

✔ KPI-driven Business Analysis

✔ Industry Benchmarking

✔ Growth & Valuation Insights

✔ Financial Risk Detection

✔ Executive-Level Dashboard

# SQL Stakeholder Analysis

A set of **25 stakeholder-driven business questions** were designed and solved.

Categories covered:

### Profitability & Margins

Industry margin leadership, ROCE analysis, profitability gaps.

### Growth Trends

Revenue sustainability and long-term expansion analysis.

### Valuation

Intrinsic valuation and market pricing opportunities.

### Financial Health

Debt stress, liquidity, and distress indicators.

### Industry Insights

Sector-level comparative analysis.

### Risk Assessment

Identification of red flags and financially vulnerable companies.
